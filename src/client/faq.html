FAQ

What is Listify?

Listify is a tool that helps you create and manage your tasks efficiently.

How do I create a new list?

To create a new list, click on the "New List" button on the dashboard and follow the instructions.

Can I share my lists with others?

Yes, you can share your lists with others by clicking the "Share" button and entering their email addresses.
